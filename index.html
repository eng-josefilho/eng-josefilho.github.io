KAUST - King Abdullah University of Science and Technology

Hello!

I am a Postdoctoral researcher at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) under the NEOM-KAUST Institute for Ocean Science and Solutions. I am working on the areas of biosensors, communication systems, prototyping, edge AI, and energy harvesting.
My research interests are in designing out-of-the-box systems to use in innovative applications. I aim at developing novel biosensor platforms and self-powered optical wireless communication systems in both underwater and in free space.
