Hello!

I am a Ph.D. student at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), working in the areas of communication systems, prototyping, and energy harvesting. I expect to receive a Ph.D. from KAUST in December 2022, under the direction of Professor Khaled N. Salama. Currently, I am also working as Software Developer for Iyris Technology, a startup company based on KAUST.
My research interests are in designing out-of-the-box systems that use well-known physical phenomenons and apply it in different applications. I aim at developing novel applications of optical wireless communication systems in both underwater and in free space.
